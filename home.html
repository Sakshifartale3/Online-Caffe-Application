ABOUT US



From Your breakfast to Breaks



We promise you a wholesome experience with delicious and healthy meals at Dreamy Treats Cafe, a trendy representation of authenticity. With your piping hot Chai, try tasty and pocket friendly dishes for breakfast, brunch, lunch, snacks and dinner. With a belief in quality and quantity, our doors open for you at 6:00 AM every morning! Hospitality has always been in our blood. Our forefathers migrated to India and since inception, got into the business of running cafes. Serving refreshing tea and bakery items was their forte. This kept passing from generation to generation and we had the inspiration in us to start a new age Dreamy Treats Cafe. Thus, the idea was born in 2016.
